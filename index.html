About

My name is Tyler S. Morren, and I am a Web Developer. I originally went to school for Civil Engineering, but during my last semester, I realized I wanted to do something different. So I started teaching myself the basics of Web Development while finishing school. I learned the basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript through Khan Academy and loved it.

Two days after I graduated with my Civil Engineering degree, I was back in school for Web Development. Since then, I have received a Web Development Certificate and have continued to learn as much as I can about the field. In school, on top of the basics, I learned how to use PHP, MySQL, jQuery, Bootstrap, and WordPress.

In my free time, I have been doing my best to keep up with current web technologies. My next goal is to learn more about AngularJS and Node.js.
